MOTORS COFFEE

PARIS 1

Adresse: 7 Rue des Halles, 75001 Paris

Description: My mother and I stumbled upon this café by chance. It was already quite busy, so after waiting in line for a little while, we were able to sit down and enjoy our coffees. The atmosphere was really cool, young, and cheerful, and we had a great time. The coffees were good and the cookies were excellent. I will definitely be back to try the other pastries and sit outside when the weather permits!

Order: 1 cappucino, 1 americano, 1 cookie

Price: 10-15 €
